Lasagna

Description: Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of wide, flat pasta sheets alternating with fillings like meat sauce (ragù), béchamel sauce, various cheeses (including ricotta, mozzarella, and Parmesan), and seasonings, all baked together in the oven until golden and then cut into squares or rectangles to serve; essentially, a layered pasta casserole.

Ingredients:

  • Meat
  • Onion and Garlic
  • Tomato Products
  • Sugar
  • Spices and Seasonings
  • Lasagana noodles
  • Cheese
  • Egg

Steps:

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagana according to the recipe instructions
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving
